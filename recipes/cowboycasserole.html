Cowboy Casserole

Look at this casserole

This casserole is made of little cowboys

If you look closely you can see their little hats

Ingredients

  • chopped bacon
  • half an onion
  • a little shake of the boot
  • some nice cheese
  • a kiss from your wife
  • ten tomatas

Steps

  1. Your gonna chop the bacon into circles
  2. take that onion and whack it down the middle
  3. stick it in that hot boot
  4. top it with the finest of your most sacred cheese
  5. turn to your wife, kiss her just right
  6. take those ten tomatos out to the bar and buy them some beers
